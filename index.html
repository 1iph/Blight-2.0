OUR STORY

A LITTLE BIT ABOUT OUR HISTORY

Blight was made by BlackVelocity on 04/11/2018. When the guild was first made, it was called Project Exodus. Around 06/06/2019, we decided to rename ourselves to Blight. Like some other guilds, we started out as a friend's group and had no intention of climbing the leaderboards. As we grew bigger, we decided to start clawing our way through the leaderboards. We started at the bottom of the list, but we grew more and more over time. Blight has always been a community guild and despite losing focus of that in certain times through various Guild Master transitions, we're now back to focusing on the heart of our guild and intend to continuously grow.

    FAQ's

    Can I join back if I'm kicked?
    ➵ If you're kicked for not meeting the Gexp requirement, you are allowed to join back after 1 week. If you continuously fail at meeting the requirement, you won't be reinvited
    How do I become a Warden?
    ➵ When we are looking for more staff, we begin a new round of applications. To apply, use the google form that will be placed in announcements when we start our round.
    What if I have a valid reason for not meeting the Gexp Requirement?
    ➵ We have an Absences system in place for people who have valid reasons such as Exams and mental health. Our general maximum length of absences is 2 weeks however this can be longer depending on the situation. If you are in the guild and want to make an absence, use the google form (I'm sure you've noticed by now that we love our google forms) which is found in our discord server or by messaging a staff member online.
    Do we hold any events?
    ➵ We hold regular events that the whole guild can participate in! Blight is primarily a community guild and we value developing bonds between our members and generally having a fun time Many events are conducted for fun however, some have prizes such as a rank upgrade. Our events range from solo Bedwars tournaments to house building competitions.
